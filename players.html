Domestic Competitions and SLFA Governance
Leone Rock Premier League (2025/26): The new season of the men's top-flight league officially began on November 8, 2025, after a Super Cup curtain-raiser on October 31, 2025. The league features 18 teams and will see a phased reduction in the number of teams in future seasons to enhance competition.
SLFA Leadership: Babadi Kamara was elected as the new SLFA President in August 2025 and has since met with government officials and potential sponsors like Africell to explore opportunities for football development. He has also championed a nationwide FIFA Football for Schools ball distribution project.
Infrastructure: A FIFA delegation concluded a successful inspection of artificial turf pitches and development projects, with the goal of improving football infrastructure across the country.
